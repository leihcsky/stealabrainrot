Home Button Strategy

The home button (G key) is your ultimate escape tool. While you can't take stolen items with you, it guarantees your safety from hammer attacks and allows you to regroup.

üö® Emergency Protocol

If you're caught in someone else's base during a counter-raid, immediately press G to teleport home. You'll lose any items you were carrying, but you'll avoid being hammered and losing health.

Practice the home button timing so it becomes muscle memory during high-stress situations.
